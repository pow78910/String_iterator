Validation conditions 

1. Original passwords - the example passwords that are in the text file 

2. Suffix OR Prefix 
	a. Numbers - general OR specific amount of numbers OR number sequences e.g. 123, 999
	b. Symbols/Special characters e.g. !£$%&*@#~ 

3. Capital letters 
	a. The beginning of the pass/word
	b. All characters
	c. most characters
	d. no characters

4. Numbers for letters
	a. 1 for 'i' and 'l'
	b. 3 for 'e' 
	c. 4 for 'A' 
	d. 5 for 's'
	e. 7 for 't' 
	f. 8 for 'B'
	g. 0 for 'o'
